Coat or Hat?

Conceptual Idea

The challenge I chose to address from the MetService lecture was to "do something different with presenting weather". An issue that was talked about was how to get more people interested in the weather - and in turn increase the users of MetService. Often people just check the weather to see what to wear and my approach is to design a service that will directly show this. This will be achieved by a mobile friendly website which instead of displaying the temperature and weather in scientific ways (e.g. degrees) will display it the in human friendly way of what to wear. Below are the three different ways this data will be displayed.

MetService would create a suggested outfit for the current location which is calculated from their scientific data and user data collected through the new app service.

The user can then see other users entries of what they are wearing spreading out from this. These entries are relevant to the current location the user is in.

And finally the user can also input their choice of outfit for the day, allowing MetService to collect data and other users to see the users decision to influence their own.
