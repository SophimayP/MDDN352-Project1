Coat or Hat?

Conceptual Idea

The challenge I chose to address from the MetService lecture was to "do something different with presenting weather". An issue that was talked about was how to get more people interested in the weather - and in turn increase the users of MetService.

My approach is to create an app which instead of displaying weather data scientifically will show it in a humanised way. Often people only check the weather to see what they should wear for the day - my pitch for is for an app that tells them what to wear rather than having to work it out from the degrees.

Conceptual Idea:

MetService would create a suggested outfit for the current location which is calculated from their scientific data and user data collected through the app service.

The user can then see other users entries of what they are wearing spreading out from this. These entries are relevant to the current location the user is in.

And finally the user can also input their choice of outfit for the day, allowing MetService to collect data and other users to see the users decision to influence their own.
